FAQ

Booking with ROYALTRANSFER is simple and convenient. You can book online through our website, call us directly at +41 76 243 25 45, or send us an email at info@royaltransport.ch. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to ensure availability.

Our transfer prices include all taxes, tolls, parking fees, and waiting time (up to 15 minutes after the scheduled pickup time). The price is fixed and there are no hidden charges. We provide professional drivers, premium vehicles, and meet & greet service.

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for airport transfers and 48 hours for special events or peak season travel. However, we can accommodate last-minute bookings subject to availability. For urgent transfers, please call us directly.

We monitor all flights and automatically adjust pickup times for delays. Our drivers will wait for you even if your flight is delayed. There are no additional charges for reasonable delays. For significant delays, please contact us to confirm your new arrival time.

We offer a premium fleet including luxury sedans, SUVs, and minivans. All our vehicles are well-maintained, clean, and equipped with modern amenities. We can accommodate groups of various sizes, from individual travelers to large groups.

Yes, we provide child seats upon request. Please specify the age and weight of your child when booking so we can provide the appropriate seat. Child seats are provided free of charge and meet all safety standards.

We accept cash (CHF, EUR, USD), credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), and bank transfers. Payment can be made in advance or to the driver upon completion of the journey. We also accept mobile payments and digital wallets.

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking free of charge up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time. For cancellations within 24 hours, a cancellation fee may apply. Please contact us as soon as possible for any changes.

Yes, we provide transfer services throughout Europe. We can arrange transfers to and from major European cities, airports, and destinations. Please contact us for specific routes and pricing for international transfers.

We are committed to providing accessible services for all passengers. We have vehicles equipped for wheelchair access and our drivers are trained to assist passengers with special needs. Please inform us of any specific requirements when booking.
